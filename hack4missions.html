    Introduction

    The Bible is one of the oldest and most popular books of all time. It’s something that everyone should be able to read in their own language, but approximately 1,600 languages around the world are still waiting for a translation project to begin.



    Design Research & User Needs

    This web app helps individuals visualize the diversity of language and the value of Bible translation. It allows users from different countries to type their names and see them instantly translated or transliterated into several languages. It utilizes an interactive React dashboard used by missionaries and churches around the world and also at Wycliffe's Discovery Center located in Orlando, Florida.


    Design Requirements

    Our prototype required us to have a website that is functional and aesthetic. The layout had to be simple in personal ways that would communicate to the user that they should type their name and discover how it is translated or transliterated in multiple languages. There needed to be a title, text box, and a statement addressing users to type their name in the text box.
