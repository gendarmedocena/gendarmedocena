About Myself

I'm Gendarme (jen-darrm), a multi-disciplinary designer focused on Visual, UI, and UX Design! I'm a recent graduate from the University of California, San Diego who majored in Cognitive Science with a specialization in Human-Computer Interaction. I have a passion for designing end-to-end user experiences!

In summer of 2018, I interned at ContinuM as a Web Developer where I redesigned 5 website features using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I spent the past winter participating in Hack4Missions, a hackathon where I teamed up and used design thinking to build mission organizations.

I specialize in everything from wireframing, iterative processing, evaluating user interfaces, prototyping, designing websites, and empathizing with my users! My goal is to show that people's lives are well understood through strong design systems!
